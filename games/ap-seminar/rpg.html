PROLOGUE
The Crucible Has Been Corrupted
For centuries, The Crucible stood as the highest test of the thinking mind. Scholars came not merely to win, but to seek truth — to strip arguments down to their foundations and rebuild them on solid ground.
But something has changed. The Sophists have taken over. They win by manipulation, not logic. By emotion, not evidence. They have turned the sacred art of argumentation into a weapon of deception.
Your mentor, the Elder Logician, tells you: "Every argument rests on a foundation. The Sophist builds on sand and hopes you don't look down. Your job is to look down — always."
You will face eight opponents, each representing a different kind of intellectual corruption. To defeat them, you must identify their fallacies, evaluate their evidence, choose the correct rebuttal — and when the moment is right, unleash an Axiom Strike: a move that cuts to the moral or logical foundation they are violating.
This is not about winning. It is about being right.
