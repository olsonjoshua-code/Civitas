PROLOGUE
The Crucible Has Been Corrupted
For centuries, The Crucible stood as the highest test of the thinking mind. Scholars came not to win — but to seek truth. To strip arguments down to their foundations and rebuild them on solid ground.
But the Sophists have taken over. They win by manipulation, not logic. By emotion, not evidence. They have turned the sacred art of argumentation into a weapon of deception.
Your mentor, the Elder Logician, tells you: "Every argument rests on a foundation. The Sophist builds on sand and hopes you don't look down. Your job is to look down — always."
You will face eight opponents, each representing a different kind of intellectual corruption. Defeat them by identifying their fallacies, evaluating their evidence, and when ready — unleash an Axiom Strike that cuts to the very foundation they are violating.
Warning: stronger opponents deal chip damage even on correct answers. You will need better weapons and armor to survive the deeper chambers of The Crucible.
