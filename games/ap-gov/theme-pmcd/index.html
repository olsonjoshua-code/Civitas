About This Game
BIG IDEA: PMCD — COMPETING POLICY-MAKING INTERESTS
Public policy emerges from conflict and compromise among many competing interests — political parties, interest groups, ideological factions, branches of government, and levels of government. No single actor controls policy outcomes. This game pulls all PMCD-tagged questions from across Units 1–5.
