About This Game
BIG IDEA: LOR — LIBERTY AND ORDER
There is an inherent tension between individual liberty — civil liberties, civil rights, personal freedom — and government's need to maintain order. Courts, legislatures, and the public must continually balance these competing values. This game pulls all LOR-tagged questions from across Units 1–5.
Topics Covered
Unit 1 — Natural rights, social contract, limited government, Declaration of Independence
Unit 2 — Executive power and security, civil liberties and wartime, congressional oversight
Unit 3 — First Amendment freedoms, due process, privacy rights, Tinker, Schenck, Gideon, Miranda
Unit 4 — Liberty as a political value, ideological debates over civil liberties
Unit 5 — Voting rights, Voting Rights Act, barriers to participation, social movements
