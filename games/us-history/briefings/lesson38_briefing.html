Lesson 38: Origins of the Cold War

    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    How did an alliance created to defeat Nazi Germany break apart so quickly after World War II, and why did Europe become the first major battleground of the Cold War?

    Key Terms

    Harry Truman
    Joseph Stalin
    Cold War
    containment

    Berlin

    What to Watch For

    1. How wartime cooperation with the Soviet Union depended on defeating Hitler, not on lasting trust.
    2. How Soviet wartime losses helped explain Stalin's demand for friendly governments in Eastern Europe.
    3. How Truman, containment, and the Marshall Plan turned American policy toward resisting communist expansion.
    4. How divided Germany and the Iron Curtain made the Cold War visible on the map of Europe.

    Study Tip

    Track the sequence in order: uneasy alliance, postwar mistrust, competing ideas about Europe, containment, Marshall aid, and the division of Germany. That timeline explains why peace turned into a Cold War instead of a stable partnership.
