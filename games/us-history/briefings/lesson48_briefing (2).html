    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    What is the proper role of government in shaping American society?

    Key Terms

    Great Society
    War on Poverty
    Economic Opportunity Act
    Medicare
    Medicaid
    Immigration Act of 1965
    Warren Court
    Miranda Rights

    What to Watch For

    1. How Johnson and Goldwater argued about liberty and federal power.
    2. Which Great Society programs attacked poverty, injustice, and lack of access.
    3. How environmental and consumer protections fit into the same reform era.
    4. Why Warren Court rulings mattered as much as Johnson's legislation.

    Study Tip

    Organize the lesson in three parts: the 1964 election debate, Johnson's domestic programs, and the Warren Court. That structure makes the details much easier to remember.
