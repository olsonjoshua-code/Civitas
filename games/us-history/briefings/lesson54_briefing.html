    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    How did the 1970s combine social change and reform with economic anxiety, political distrust, and a growing sense of national uncertainty?

    Key Terms

    Me Decade
    Stagflation
    Sunbelt
    Environmentalism
    OPEC
    Energy Crisis
    Camp David Accords
    Iran Hostage Crisis

    What to Watch For

    1. How changing work, family life, and migration patterns reshaped American society.
    2. How environmental reform and new technology suggested change and possibility.
    3. How oil shocks, inflation, and gas lines fed the mood of malaise.
    4. How Carter mixed real foreign-policy success with a deepening image of weakness.

    Study Tip

    Split the lesson into two tracks: social change and national anxiety. Once you connect those two stories, the phrase "Me Decade" starts to feel less like a slogan and more like a description of the whole era.
