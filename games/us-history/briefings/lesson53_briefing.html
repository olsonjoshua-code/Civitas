    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    How could Richard Nixon achieve major policy successes and still destroy his own presidency?

    Key Terms

    New Federalism
    Silent Majority
    Detente
    China Opening
    SALT I
    OSHA
    Oil Embargo
    Watergate

    What to Watch For

    1. How Nixon rebuilt his career and won the presidency in 1968.
    2. How his domestic and foreign policy record was more complicated than a simple conservative label suggests.
    3. How the energy crisis and economic strain hurt public confidence.
    4. How Watergate undid the authority Nixon had spent decades building.

    Study Tip

    Split the lesson into two halves: rise and governing success, then scandal and collapse. The contrast between those halves is the whole point of Nixon's story.
