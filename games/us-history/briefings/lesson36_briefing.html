    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    What military strategies did the United States and its allies pursue to defeat the Axis powers in World War II?

    Key Terms

    Allies
    Axis powers
    Battle of Midway
    Battle of Okinawa
    Battle of Stalingrad
    D-Day
    Holocaust
    Manhattan Project

    What to Watch For

    1. How the Allies chose a "Europe First" strategy and what that meant for the Pacific.
    2. How the war unfolded in North Africa, Italy, and France before reaching Germany.
    3. How the Holocaust was carried out and what Allied forces discovered as they advanced.
    4. How the United States stopped Japan in the Pacific and what decisions led to the atomic bomb.

    Study Tip

    Keep two maps in your head at once: Europe and the Pacific. Track the Allied advance on both fronts together, and ask yourself at each step why the Allies made the choices they did—geography, resources, and timing all shaped the strategy.
