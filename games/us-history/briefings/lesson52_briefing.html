    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    How did the United States try to end the Vietnam War, and why did the exit remain so costly and chaotic?

    Key Terms

    Vietnamization
    Cambodia
    Laos
    My Lai Massacre
    Kent State
    Paris Peace Accords
    War Powers Resolution
    Fall of Saigon

    What to Watch For

    1. How Nixon reduced American troops while still widening the war in Southeast Asia.
    2. How bombing, Cambodia, and protest kept the war politically explosive.
    3. How Paris Peace brought U.S. withdrawal without bringing real peace to Vietnam.
    4. How Saigon's collapse exposed the limits of "peace with honor."

    Study Tip

    Build a simple timeline: Vietnamization, Cambodia and protest, the Paris Peace Accords, and the fall of Saigon. This lesson works best when you see it as one long, difficult exit rather than a set of separate events.
