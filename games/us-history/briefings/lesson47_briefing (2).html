    What to Watch For

    1. How image and symbolism shaped Kennedy's reputation.
    2. Where Kennedy succeeded and where he struggled.
    3. Why Cold War events defined so much of his presidency.
    4. How assassination changed the way Americans remembered him.

    Study Tip

    Don't just memorize events. Keep asking: what did Kennedy actually accomplish, and what part of his reputation came from image, hope, and tragedy?
