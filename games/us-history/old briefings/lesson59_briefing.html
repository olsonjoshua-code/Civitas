    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    How have Americans continued to debate the meaning of liberty, equality, and democracy in recent years?

    Key Terms

    founding ideals
    Tinker standard
    voter access
    Obergefell v. Hodges
    DACA
    equal protection
    Black Lives Matter
    civil liberties

    What to Watch For

    1. How courts and social movements both shape the meaning of freedom and equality.
    2. How immigration, marriage, voting, and protest all raise questions about who fully belongs in American democracy.
    3. How the same founding ideals can be used by different groups to argue opposite positions.
    4. How modern conflicts show that the Constitution is still being interpreted, not simply repeated.

    Study Tip

    For each example, ask two things: which founding ideal is at stake, and which institution is driving the debate: the courts, voters, government, or protest movements. That turns a long list of events into one clear pattern.
