    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    How did the counterculture challenge mainstream American values in the 1960s?

    Key Terms

    Counterculture
    Mainstream Culture
    Free Speech Movement
    Hippies
    Communes
    Psychedelic Rock
    Pop Art
    Woodstock

    What to Watch For

    1. Why many young Americans rejected suburban conformity and consumer values.
    2. How student protest opened the door to wider cultural rebellion.
    3. Why music, art, clothing, and lifestyle became political statements.
    4. How Woodstock symbolized both the peak and limits of the counterculture.

    Study Tip

    Compare mainstream America and the counterculture side by side. If you track differences in values, style, music, and politics, the whole lesson becomes easier to organize.
