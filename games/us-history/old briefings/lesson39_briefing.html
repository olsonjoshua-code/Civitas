    Study Guide

    Essential Question

    Were the methods used by the United States to contain communism justified?

    Key Terms

    Berlin Blockade
    NATO
    Warsaw Pact
    Korean War
    H-bomb
    Third World
    brinkmanship
    MAD

    What to Watch For

    1. How the Berlin Blockade and airlift set the tone for Cold War confrontation in Europe.
    2. How NATO and the Warsaw Pact formally divided Europe into two armed camps.
    3. How communism's spread to China and the Korean War turned the Cold War global.
    4. How the hydrogen bomb and the doctrine of Mutual Assured Destruction kept the world on the brink.

    Study Tip

    Think of the Cold War as a chess match played on multiple boards at once — Europe, Asia, and the Third World — while both players held nuclear pieces neither dared to use. Track where each "move" happened and why.
